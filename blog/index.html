The Cause of Trumpism - The Remedy

Mar 23, 2025

Free Democracies can only stand so long as they provide for their people. Allowing citizens to languish and struggle under inadequate material conditions can lead them to seek salvation in unsavory places. Let us remember to whom our obligation is, and what we have to loose if we don’t fulfill it.

How to Tax for Prosperity

Mar 15, 2025

Taxes must be thought as more than simply a necessary evil. They are amongst the most powerful tool in a governments arsenal to affect change. We should levy taxes with a singular focus: How does this change the incentive structure of economic behavior?

Inflation Is a Lie

When media pundits and politicians talk about inflation, they are almost always lying to you. They use the word one way when discussing the problem, and a very different way when discussing the solution.

Our Relationship with the Police

Jan 10, 2025

Police forces are publicly furnished organizations for which we allow to have a monopoly on legal violence. The cost of that privilege should be a significantly higher bar of expectation of honor, integrity, and conformation to the law. We must remember that they are public servants bound to Protect & Serve.

Housing Cost is the Driver of Economic Struggle

Jan 5, 2025

The most effective thing we can do for all our problems is reduce the cost of housing. It is the root of all aspects of affordability. For everything you purchase, it is made more expensive by the need to pay for more expensive than necessary housing for all the people involved in providing the good to you.
