While I am running in District 30B, I am running to solve problems for the entirety of Maryland. We have issues that matter to us most, but the fundamental issues that risk our stability as a State, and as a Nation, are common across Maryland, and the country.

I was born and raised in Maryland and I want to fight to see us become the best place to live for working families.
